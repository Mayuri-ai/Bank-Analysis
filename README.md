1. Project Title: Sales Analytics Dashboard
2. Objective:

To analyze and monitor sales performance across different countries, segments, products, and discount bands, enabling management to make data-driven business decisions.

3. Key Insights & Metrics Displayed:

Sum of Sales: $118.72M

Sum of COGS: $101.83M

Total Margin: $16.89M

Profit %: 14.23%

Average Sales per Order: 169,609

4. Dashboard Components:

Total Sales by Country:
Bar chart showing sales distribution across countries such as the USA, Canada, France, Germany, and Mexico.

Total Sales by Discount Band:
Donut chart visualizing sales share based on discount levels (High, Medium, Low, None).

Total Sales by Segment:
Pie chart analyzing sales contributions from segments like Government, Small Business, Enterprise, and Midmarket.

Total Sales by Product:
Horizontal bar chart listing product-wise sales performance (Paseo, VTT, Velo, Amarilla, Montana, Carrera).

5. Interactive Filters: Country, Segment ,Product, Discount Band ,ate Range Selector
6. Tools & Techniques Used:

Tool: Microsoft Power BI

Data Transformation: Power Query Editor

Modeling: Relationship building and DAX measures for KPIs (Sales, COGS, Profit %, Margin).

Visualization: Bar charts, Donut charts, and interactive slicers for enhanced filtering.

7. Outcome / Impact:

Provided a clear view of sales trends and profitability by region, segment, and product.

Helped management identify top-performing markets and discount strategies.
